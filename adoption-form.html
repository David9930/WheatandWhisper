Sovereignty Grace Golden Retrievers

Thank you for your interest in welcoming one of our golden babies into your home! This application helps us ensure the best match between puppies and families. Please answer all questions thoroughly and honestly.

Applicant Information

Home Environment

Note: Written permission from landlord required for approval

Note: Physical fence is strongly preferred for puppies

Household Members

Children must be gentle and supervised with puppies

Dog Experience & Current Pets

Note: We will contact your veterinarian to verify vaccination history and quality of care

Lifestyle & Time Commitment

Puppies require frequent attention, especially in the first 6 months

We strongly recommend crate training for housebreaking and safety

Goldens are active dogs requiring 1-2 hours of exercise daily

Understanding of Golden Retrievers

Note: Puppies are placed on temperament, not color preference

Goldens require brushing 3-4 times per week minimum

Financial Responsibility

Estimated Annual Costs for a Golden Retriever:

  • Food & Treats: $800-1,500
  • Routine Veterinary Care: $500-1,000
  • Heartworm/Flea Prevention: $200-400
  • Grooming: $300-800
  • Training Classes: $300-600
  • Toys, Supplies, Misc: $300-500
  • Total: $2,400-4,800 per year
  • Emergency fund recommended: $2,000-5,000

Puppy Preferences

Temperament is more important than gender for a family pet

Note: We match based on temperament and family needs, not color
