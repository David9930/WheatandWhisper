Your New Golden

Please take a moment to review your last response. What you've written to me is a detailed description of your ideal golden. Please take note that goldens take on average 2-3 years to fully mature mentally and physically. Your "ideal image" of that beautiful, well-mannered golden retriever is years away. If you are not willing to put in some work during the puppy stage to reach your above goal, I strongly suggest you reconsider moving forward in my application and seek to adopt an adult dog instead. Future Plans

Goldens do best in life when they have a purpose. They were not bred to be couch potatoes. They were initially bred to work all day in the field with hunters, retrieving downed birds on land and in water. They have drive, energy, and a desire to play and please. These dogs love people and love the outdoors. They make wonderful family dogs. You may discover a whole new world of fun! Some of my best memories bonding with my dogs was during hikes and traveling.
