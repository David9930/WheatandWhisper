Bits and pieces of our life

Sharing my thoughts through the seasons as we live, learn, and grow here at the farm... our goal is to show up each day with a desire to "Honor Jesus and be prudent with what He has given us, to respect the land and the animals, to love abundant love and joy, and to be present for those full day we're been blessed with.

Emotions

There are brief moments of this little farmette "in the beauty of it all", the moments of ups and downs, joy and heartache, and the one those chosen to live, the life we have poured into and built for our family... a joy that takes immense responsibility and time. It's something we would never trade... as we see the benefit this lifestyle has for our children. Something about the joy of each new season, the new life and births in the spring, the colors of fall, the quiet of winter, and the serenity of it all.

It keeps us grounded. It keeps us steady. It keeps us full. It keeps us in awe of God's creation and wonder.

Vision

Vision is such a powerful thing to think about. We often make plans, create goals, chase dreams... but vision, the ability to think about the future with imagination and direction... that is something to be so mindful of. I find myself often imagining what my children will remember most about their childhood, what I will remember most about my motherhood, and what things we will have created more of or less of. Our vision now is so clear: The days are long but the years are short, and we need to embrace every opportunity of a new day with grace, softness, and expectancy of God's plan.
