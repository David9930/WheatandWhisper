Welcome

Keeping life golden

We are a family in love with golden retrievers and we breed high quality, fully health tested, AKC Goldens. All of our dogs are fully registered, have all of their OFAs, and full genetic panels. We are also proud members of the Golden Retriever Club of America.

Certifications and Testing

responsible breeding is everything:

  • full genetic panel
  • echocardiogram
  • hip and elbow x-rays
  • annual eye evaluations
  • veterinary exams
  • AKC registrations

Our Breeding Program

Quality over quantity

We focus on breeding healthy, well-tempered Golden Retrievers that make perfect family companions. Each puppy is raised in our home with love and care, ensuring they're well-socialized and ready for their forever families.

484-354-5866 || Paige.grauch@gmail.com

Puppy Care

From birth to forever home

Our puppies receive:

  • Veterinary health checks
  • Age-appropriate vaccinations
  • Deworming treatments
  • Early socialization
  • Microchipping
  • AKC registration papers
